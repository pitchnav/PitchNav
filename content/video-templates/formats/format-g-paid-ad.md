# Format G — Paid ad

**Best for:** paid Instagram Reel ads. This is the format used by all five launch ads and the flagship ad.
**Default length:** 30s (see `content/video-templates/timelines/30s.md`).

## Structure

1. Result-oriented hook — leads with the outcome or observation the viewer cares about, in the first 2 seconds
2. Real pitching footage — immediate, not a logo animation or a slow build
3. Quick problem reveal — the mechanical observation, shown fast
4. Report output — real dashboard/report screen recording
5. Three key benefits — stated plainly, each tied to something the product actually does:
   - a clear mechanics breakdown from one video
   - a personalized priority and drills
   - progress tracking over time
6. Price or offer — "$25/month" only when the ad's objective is direct conversion; omit for upper-funnel awareness variants
7. CTA — "Start your Pitch Nav analysis."

## Rules specific to this format

- Message clarity within the first 2 seconds is non-negotiable for paid placements — see `content/exports/export-presets.md`.
- Brand (logo/wordmark) should be visible early but never before the hook.
- Product must be shown before the halfway point of the runtime.
- Exactly one CTA, clearly visible at the end.
- Price appears only when appropriate — do not force "$25/month" into every paid ad variant (see `content/video-campaigns/` for which of the five launch ads include it).

## Footage needed

- Real full-speed + slow-motion pitching footage
- Real report/dashboard screen recording
- Brand asset: `public/pitch-nav-logo-source.png` (minimal use — end card only, not a persistent watermark)

## Caption rhythm example

```
THIS LOOKS LIKE A GOOD DELIVERY
UNTIL YOU STOP IT HERE
ONE VIDEO. A FULL BREAKDOWN.
PRIORITY. DRILLS. PROGRESS.
START YOUR ANALYSIS
```
