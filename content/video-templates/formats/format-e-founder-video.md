# Format E — Founder video

**Best for:** founder-led promotional videos, credibility-building organic Reels, top-of-funnel paid ads.
**Default length:** 30s–45s.

## Structure

1. Direct-to-camera hook — founder speaking straight to camera, no slow intro, the hook line delivered in the first 2 seconds
2. Founder problem statement — the real reason Pitch Nav exists, stated plainly
3. Product reason — how Pitch Nav specifically addresses that problem
4. Quick platform demo — a few seconds of real product screen recording, not described, shown
5. Outcome — what a pitcher walks away with (a clear breakdown, priorities, drills, a plan — not a guaranteed result)
6. CTA — "Start your Pitch Nav analysis."

## Rules specific to this format

- Direct-to-camera footage must be real founder footage. This format does not use AI-generated presenters.
- Keep the problem statement specific and credible — avoid generic founder-story clichés.
- The demo beat should show, not narrate over a blank screen — cut to the real dashboard/report while speaking.
- Tone stays professional sports-tech, not salesy; confidence without hype.

## Footage needed

- Direct-to-camera founder footage (from `content/shot-lists/founder-footage-checklist.md`)
- Real product screen recording for the demo beat
- Optional: B-roll of the founder reviewing an actual report or analyzing footage, for the outcome beat

```
I BUILT THIS FOR ONE REASON
MOST FEEDBACK IS TOO VAGUE
PITCH NAV SHOWS THE DELIVERY CLEARLY
UPLOAD. BREAKDOWN. PRIORITIES. DRILLS.

```
