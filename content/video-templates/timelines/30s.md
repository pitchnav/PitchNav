# 30-second timeline (default)

This is the default structure for launch ads, paid Instagram ads, and the
flagship ad. Every script format in `content/video-templates/formats/` maps
onto this timeline unless a shorter/longer variant is specified.

See `_pacing-techniques.md` for the technique menu referenced below.

| Timecode | Beat | Must be true on screen | Suggested technique |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0:00–0:02 | Pattern-interrupt hook | Strongest visual + hook caption, no logo animation, no slow intro | Hard cut on action |
| 0:02–0:05 | Immediate visual proof | Real pitch footage that makes good on the hook's promise | Slow motion in, punch-in zoom |
| 0:05–0:10 | Reveal the problem or surprising observation | The specific mechanical detail, shown, not just described | Freeze frame + joint-marker animation |
| 0:10–0:15 | Build a curiosity loop or show the repeated pattern | Second rep, second angle, or a "watch this again" callback | Split screen, angle overlay |
| 0:15–0:21 | Deliver the useful coaching insight or solution | The one idea worth remembering, plus the drill if there's room | Diagram insert, caption |
| 0:21–0:26 | Show Pitch Nav product output | Dashboard, report, or priority screen — unmistakably the real product | UI screen recording |
| 0:26–0:30 | One clear CTA | "Start your Pitch Nav analysis." Price only if the format calls for it | Static end card, logo small and bottom-safe |

**Notes:** This is the length used for all five launch-ad variants and the
flagship ad. Keep exactly one CTA — do not stack "follow us" or "comment
below" on top of the analysis CTA.
