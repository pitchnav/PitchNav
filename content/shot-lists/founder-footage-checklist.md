# Founder footage checklist

Print this or open it on a phone during the shoot. Check off each item as
you capture it. Shoot every item even if that day's video only needs one —
founder footage is expensive to re-shoot and cheap to have extra of.

- [ ] Direct-to-camera hook — 5-6 short takes of different hook lines from
      `content/video-templates/hook-library.json` (founder-and-credibility
      category), each delivered as its own clean take
- [ ] Side-angle explanation — founder explaining a concept from a 3/4 angle,
      for B-roll under voice-over
- [ ] Website demonstration — founder's hands/screen navigating the real
      site, or founder narrating while a screen recording plays
- [ ] Report demonstration — founder pointing at or discussing a real report
      on a laptop/tablet
- [ ] Behind-the-scenes analysis — founder reviewing footage, marking a
      priority, general "at work" B-roll
- [ ] Camera setup explanation — founder demonstrating the 15-feet/6-feet-high
      setup described in `content/video-templates/formats/format-c-camera-mistake.md`

## Shoot notes

- Wardrobe: solid colors, no busy patterns (moiré on compressed video),
  nothing that matches the navy background exactly
- Lighting: even, no harsh shadows; avoid backlighting from a window
- Audio: lav mic if available; room tone recorded for 10 seconds at the end
- Get at least 3 takes of every hook line — energy varies take to take and
  you want options in the edit
