Companion data file: `content/video-campaigns/flagship-understand-your-delivery.campaign.json`

This is the hero Pitch Nav ad — the one creative every other launch ad is a
variation of. It should be the most polished, most restrained piece in the
system: premium sports-tech, not loud.

**Target audience:** Broad primary audience — competitive pitchers 13-18 and their parents, plus pitching coaches evaluating the product.

## Footage needed

- Real full-speed + slow-motion side-view pitch, one athlete, two reps, both matched to foot strike
- Real, moving screen recording of the dashboard/report: priority → drills → progress calendar (not a static screenshot)
- `public/pitch-nav-logo-source.png` for the end card

## Visual effects

Blue overlay (AI-generated motion graphic over real footage) highlighting the rotation-vs-stability pattern at foot strike, on both reps identically so the comparison reads instantly. No other graphic treatment — this ad is restrained by design.

## Sound design

Premium, low-key music bed, ducked under voice-over throughout, building slightly into the final card. One whoosh on the speed ramp into slow motion. Two freeze-frame stop accents (rep 1 and rep 2) — keep them identical so the repetition itself reads as intentional. No glitch effects, no screen shake.

## CTA

"Start your Pitch Nav analysis." — spoken and on screen, paired with "Understand your delivery."

## Cover text / thumbnail concept

First-frame text: **THIS LOOKS LIKE A GOOD DELIVERY…** over the real pitch mid-windup. The incomplete sentence (ellipsis) is the hook mechanism — it should look identical to a normal highlight clip until the caption creates doubt.

## Social caption

Full speed, this looks like a good delivery. Stop it at the right frame and the sequence tells a different story — twice. This is exactly what Pitch Nav's mechanics breakdown is built to catch. Start your Pitch Nav analysis.
