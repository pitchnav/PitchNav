## Footage needed

- Real founder direct-to-camera footage (see `content/shot-lists/founder-footage-checklist.md`) — no AI-generated presenter

## Visual effects

Cut from founder to pitch footage at 0:02 (hard cut, not a dissolve). Blue joint-marker overlay, split screen, speed ramp, freeze frame — same technique set as the other launch ads for the shared middle.

## Sound design

Music bed enters after the founder's opening line, ducked below VO throughout. One whoosh, one tick, one soft overlay accent.

## CTA

"Start your Pitch Nav analysis." — spoken and on screen.

## Alternative ending

Return to the founder on camera for the final 4 seconds instead of a static end card — founder delivers the CTA line directly, wordmark appears beside them. Test against the standard end card for trust-driven audiences.

## Paid-ad variation

Includes "$25/MONTH" in the final card. "Learn More" button routes to `/start-analysis`. Strongest candidate for warm/retargeting audiences who have already seen a feature-first ad.

## Organic variation

Drop "$25/MONTH" — CTA reads "START YOUR ANALYSIS" only, with "Link in bio" added beneath it. This hook performs well as a standalone organic post independent of the paid campaign — treat it as a Format E (Founder Video) candidate too.
